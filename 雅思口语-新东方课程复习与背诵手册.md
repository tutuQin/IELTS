## 一、新东方课程的核心体系

新东方课程的重点不是提供无限话题答案，而是建立一套可以迁移到不同题目的答题系统：

```text
Part 1：Answer + Further Information

Part 2：按人物、地点、事件、物品分类组织素材

Part 3：识别题型 → 明确立场 → 解释 → 举例/对比 → 结果或解决方案
```

课程中所有方法最终都服务于两个目标：

1. `speak at length`：能够持续、充分地表达；
2. 用有逻辑的内容展示词汇、语法和发音能力。

### 必须牢记的总原则

- 先回答问题，不要先说空洞背景；
- 回答之后必须补进一步信息；
- 形容词最好用动作或事实证明；
- 并列信息要分主次；
- Part 2 使用清楚的描述顺序；
- Part 3 先明确观点，再展开社会层面的分析；
- 方法可以复用，答案内容必须个性化。

## 二、考试结构与评分标准

### 1. 考试三部分

| 部分 | 内容 | 时长 | 训练目标 |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Part 1 | 工作/学习、住所、家乡及日常话题 | 4–5分钟 | 每题2–4句，直接而自然 |
| Part 2 | 1分钟准备，最长约2分钟个人陈述 | 3–4分钟 | 有顺序、有细节、有结尾 |
| Part 3 | 与 Part 2 相关的抽象讨论 | 4–5分钟 | 观点、原因、例子、平衡与结果 |

### 2. 四项评分

#### Fluency and Coherence

重点：

- 能否持续表达；
- 是否因寻找基础词汇而频繁停顿；
- 观点是否有逻辑连接；
- 是否会使用停顿、指代和连接词管理答案。

背诵句：

- `The main reason is that…`
- `Another point worth mentioning is…`
- `As a result, …`
- `That said, …`

#### Lexical Resource

重点：

- 话题词汇是否够用；
- 搭配是否自然；
- 是否能做同义改写和忘词转述；
- 是否适量使用不那么常见但自然的表达。

不要机械替换：

| 基础词 | 可选升级 | 注意 |
|---|---|---|
| important | significant / essential / vital | 不必每次都升级 |
| good | excellent / impressive / rewarding | 根据具体意义选择 |
| bad | harmful / unpleasant / ineffective | 避免笼统 |
| big | large / enormous / considerable | 与名词搭配要自然 |

#### Grammatical Range and Accuracy

重点：

- 基础句准确；
- 时态能反映真实时间关系；
- 会使用定语从句、条件句、原因和让步从句；
- 复杂句不会破坏流利度。

#### Pronunciation

重点：

- 整体是否容易理解；
- 单词重音、句子重音和意群是否清楚；
- 语调是否帮助表达态度；
- 个别音错误不能持续影响理解。

### 3. 高分不等于什么

- 不等于语速很快；
- 不等于每句都有高级词；
- 不等于每题使用固定模板；
- 不等于观点一定非常深奥；
- 不等于必须说英音或美音；
- 不等于背诵别人完整的人生故事。

## 三、核心方法：Answer + Further Information

### 1. 基本公式

```text
Answer + Further Information
```

先给答案，再补：

- 原因；
- 例子；
- 细节；
- 经历；
- 对比；
- 结果；
- 感受。

### 2. 六种进一步信息

| 类型 | 自问 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 原因 | Why? | mainly because… |
| 例子 | What example? | For example, … |
| 细节 | What exactly? | In particular, … |
| 经历 | When? | I remember a time when… |
| 对比 | Compared with? | Unlike…, … |
| 结果 | So what? | As a result, … |

### 3. 动词优先于空泛形容词

只说：

> My father is brilliant.

信息不足。用行为证明：

> My father is extremely patient. Whenever I have a problem, he listens carefully and helps me work through it instead of simply telling me what to do.

背诵结构：

```text
He/She is one of the most [形容词] people I know.
Whenever [情境], he/she [具体行为].
```

### 4. Part 1 基础示例

题目：Do you like your hometown?

> Yes, definitely. The main reason is that the people there are friendly and welcoming. On top of that, the city has a lot of local food that I really enjoy, especially **[具体食物]**.

题目：Do you enjoy travelling?

> Yes, I do. Travelling gives me a chance to experience different cultures and get away from my daily routine. I normally travel with family or close friends because shared experiences bring us closer together.

### 5. 必须背熟的核心句干

- The main reason is that…
- This is mainly because…
- What I like most about it is…
- One thing worth mentioning is…
- For example, …
- As a result, …
- It makes me feel…
- Compared with…, …

替换练习：每个句干至少用于5个话题。

## 四、五种通用展开方法

## 方法一：主次法

### 适用情况

答案中存在多个原因、活动、特点或事物。

### 结构

```text
主要内容详细展开
→ 次要内容一句补充
```

### 背诵句干

- The main reason is that…
- What matters most to me is…
- On top of that, …
- Another, less important reason is…

### 示例

题目：Why do you like your hometown?

> The main reason is that my family and most of my close friends live there, so I always feel a strong sense of belonging. On top of that, the local food is excellent, particularly **[食物]**.

### 练习要求

不要把三个原因说成没有解释的清单。至少详细解释一个。

## 方法二：举例法

### 适用情况

自己提到复数名词，例如 activities、places、problems、benefits。

### 结构

```text
列举2–3个例子 → 选择其中一个展开
```

### 背诵句干

- There are several…, such as A, B and C.
- The one I like most is…
- Take **[例子]** as an example.
- A good example would be…

### 示例

> There are several outdoor activities people can do in my hometown, such as hiking, cycling and having picnics. Hiking is probably the most popular because there are a number of trails just outside the city.

## 方法三：前—中—后法

### 适用情况

问题、改变、困难和解决方案。

### 结构

```text
前：原因或背景
中：问题、过程或影响
后：解决办法或结果
```

### 背诵句干

- This is mainly due to…
- As a result, …
- This can lead to…
- The government/individuals could…

### 示例

> Traffic has become much worse because more families can afford private cars. As a result, rush-hour journeys take longer and air pollution has increased. The city could improve public transport and provide safer cycling routes.

## 方法四：正反验证法

### 适用情况

Yes/No、agree/disagree、A or B 等立场题。

### 结构

```text
明确立场 → 两个支持理由 → 一句让步或反面对比
```

### 背诵句干

- I strongly believe that…
- One reason is that…
- Another reason is…
- That said, I can understand why…
- Even though…, it does not change the fact that…

### 示例

> I believe students should choose subjects they are genuinely interested in. Interest makes them more willing to work hard, and it also helps them remain motivated when the course becomes difficult. That said, advice from parents and teachers can still be valuable.

## 方法五：中立法

### 适用情况

答案真正取决于年龄、地点、经济条件或个人情况。

### 背诵句干

- It depends, really.
- It varies from person to person.
- For some people…, whereas for others…
- This may be true in…, but not necessarily in…

### 示例

> It depends on the child’s age. Younger children may benefit from clear rules, whereas teenagers usually need more freedom to learn how to manage their own time.

### 使用限制

不要每题都说 `It depends`。能明确站边时优先给出明确观点。

## 五、Part 1 答题与背诵

### 1. 推荐结构

```text
直接回答 + 原因/细节 + 可选例子或感受
```

### 2. 肯定、否定和不确定

| 功能 | 表达 |
|---|---|
| 肯定 | Yes, definitely. / Absolutely. / I do, actually. |
| 否定 | Not really. / Not at all. / I wouldn’t say so. |
| 不确定 | I’m not entirely sure. / It depends. / It varies. |

### 3. 喜欢与不喜欢

喜欢：

- be really into…
- be keen on…
- be fond of…
- be a big fan of…
- What I like most is…

不喜欢：

- not be particularly keen on…
- It’s not really my thing.
- What puts me off is…
- I can’t say I’m a fan of…

### 4. 工作/学习范例

> I’m currently working as a **[职业]**. My day-to-day work mainly involves **[职责1]** and **[职责2]**. It can be demanding when I have a tight deadline, but overall I find it challenging and rewarding.

> I’m studying **[专业]** at the moment. I chose it mainly because **[原因]**. What I enjoy most is **[课程/技能]**, although **[困难]** can be quite challenging.

### 5. 住所范例

> I live in a **[flat/house]** with **[人]**. It isn’t particularly large, but it is bright and comfortable. My favourite room is **[房间]** because it gives me a quiet place to relax or concentrate.

### 6. 家乡范例

> I’m from **[城市]**, which is located in **[位置]**. It is best known for **[食物/景点/产业]**. What I like most is that the locals are friendly and welcoming, although traffic has become heavier in recent years.

### 7. 业余活动范例

> I’m really into **[爱好]**, and I’ve been doing it for **[时间]**. I normally do it **[频率]** because it helps me unwind and gives me a sense of achievement.

### 8. Part 1 背诵任务

必须能脱离笔记回答：

1. What do you do?
2. Why did you choose your job or subject?
3. What kind of home do you live in?
4. What do you like about your hometown?
5. What do you do in your free time?
6. What is your morning routine?
7. What kind of music or weather do you like?
8. What would you like to do in the future?

每题准备：直接答案、一个原因、一个具体细节。

## 六、Part 2 人物类

## 1. 普通人物框架

```text
Identity → Appearance → Personality
→ Relationship → Influence → Ending
```

### Appearance

可用词：

- tall / short / slim / fit / well-built；
- have a warm smile；
- look younger than one’s age；
- dress neatly / casually；
- have bright/gentle eyes。

背诵句：

- He/She is fairly tall and **[身材]**.
- He/She has a warm smile, which makes him/her look approachable.
- He/She tends to dress **[风格]**.

### Personality

可用词：

- friendly / approachable / easygoing；
- warm-hearted / thoughtful / reliable；
- outgoing / reserved；
- determined / patient；
- short-tempered / self-centred。

必须用事例证明：

> She is one of the most thoughtful people I know. Whenever someone is going through a difficult time, she checks in on them and offers practical help.

### Relationship

- have a close relationship with…；
- get on/along well with…；
- have a lot in common；
- have similar tastes in music, films or sport；
- do not always see eye to eye。

### Influence

- have a positive influence on…；
- change the way I…；
- I used to…, but now…；
- teach me the importance of…。

### 普通人物完整示范

> The person I’d like to talk about is my father. He is fairly tall and well-built, and he has a warm smile that makes him look very approachable.
>
> In terms of personality, he is one of the most patient people I know. Whenever I make a mistake, he listens to my explanation and helps me find a solution instead of losing his temper. We get on more like friends than father and son because we have a lot in common, particularly our interest in films and chess.
>
> He has had a strong influence on my attitude towards other people. I used to be rather impatient and focused mainly on myself, but he taught me to listen and consider other people’s feelings. That is the main reason I admire him.

### 必背语块

- one of the most…people I know；
- whenever…, he/she…；
- get on more like friends；
- have a lot in common；
- have a positive influence on；
- I used to…, but now…。

## 2. 名人框架

```text
Background → How you knew them → Appearance/personality
→ Achievement → Influence
```

### Background

- He/She is a well-known **[职业]** from **[国家]**.
- I first heard about him/her when…
- I got to know his/her work through…

### Achievement

- be recognised for…；
- receive/win an award；
- make a major contribution to…；
- become widely known for…。

### Influence

- What I admire most is…；
- I regard him/her as a role model because…；
- His/Her work changed the way I think about…；
- He/She encouraged me to…。

### 名人示范

> A public figure I admire is **[人物]**, who is widely known for **[领域]**. I first learned about him/her through **[渠道]** when I was **[时间]**.
>
> What I admire most is not only the person’s achievement in **[领域]**, but also **[性格/社会贡献]**. For example, **[具体行为]**. This made a strong impression on me because it showed that success can be used to help other people.
>
> I regard this person as a role model because he/she encouraged me to become more **[品质]** and to take **[事情]** more seriously.

## 七、Part 2 地点类

### 1. 描述框架

```text
What and where → Surroundings and appearance
→ Activities/background → Why you like it → Feeling
```

### 2. 位置

- be located/situated in…；
- lie/stand in…；
- be close to…；
- be within walking distance of…；
- be surrounded by…；
- be away from the hustle and bustle of the city。

例句：

> It is located near the city centre and is within walking distance of a bus stop.

> It is surrounded by trees and is far enough from the main road to remain peaceful.

### 3. 外观和环境

- come in a modern/traditional style；
- be decorated with…；
- spacious / compact / vast / crowded；
- bright / peaceful / well-maintained；
- have plenty of green space。

### 4. 活动

- It is an ideal place for **doing…**
- It is a great place to **do…**
- People go there to…
- What I normally do there is…

### 5. 感受

- Whenever I go there, I feel…
- The moment I arrive, …
- It helps me get away from…
- It always leaves me feeling refreshed.

### 地点完整示范

> A place I often visit is a public park near my home. It is located close to the city centre, but it is separated from the main road by a large area of trees, so it is surprisingly peaceful.
>
> The park is not enormous, but it is well-maintained and has walking paths, open lawns and a small lake. It is an ideal place for walking, jogging or simply sitting outside with friends. I normally go there at weekends because it only takes me about fifteen minutes to walk there.
>
> The main reason I like it is that it gives me a break from screens and traffic. Whenever I spend an hour there, I feel calmer and more refreshed.

### 必背语块

- be located in；
- be surrounded by；
- within walking distance of；
- an ideal place for doing；
- The main reason I like it is…；
- leave me feeling refreshed。

## 八、Part 2 事件类

### 1. 6W框架

```text
What / When / Where / Who / Why / How
```

故事结构：

```text
Background → Beginning → Development
→ Turning point → Result → Feeling/lesson
```

### 2. 主要时态

- 一般过去时：推进事件；
- 过去进行时：描述背景；
- 过去完成时：说明更早的事情；
- 现在时：最后评价现在的影响。

### 3. 常用路标

- It happened **[时间]** when…
- At the time, I was…
- What happened was…
- A little later…
- The turning point came when…
- In the end…
- Looking back, …

### 4. 时间介词

| 介词 | 用途 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| on | 具体日期或一天 | on Monday / on my birthday |
| at | 时间点 | at 7 p.m. / at noon |
| in | 月、年、季节、时间段 | in July / in 2025 / in the morning |
| by | 截止时间 | by the end of the day |

### 5. 感受和意义

- It was a memorable experience because…
- It meant a great deal to me.
- It was a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
- It changed the way I think about…
- I became more…as a result.

### 事件完整示范

> An event I remember clearly is my cousin’s wedding, which took place in a hotel in the city centre a few years ago. I went there with my parents, and most of our extended family attended.
>
> When we arrived, everyone was taking photographs and talking excitedly. The ceremony itself was quite simple, but the atmosphere was warm and festive. The moment I remember most clearly was when the couple exchanged rings and thanked both families for supporting them.
>
> After the ceremony, we had dinner and spent several hours talking to relatives I had not seen for a long time. Looking back, the wedding was memorable not because it was expensive or dramatic, but because it brought the whole family together.

### 必背语块

- take place；
- the atmosphere was…；
- the moment I remember most clearly；
- exchange rings；
- bring the family together；
- Looking back, …。

## 九、Part 2 物品类

### 1. PDFM框架

```text
P — Purpose：如何得到、为什么需要
D — Description：外观、价格、功能
F — Feelings：使用体验
M — Meaning：情感和象征意义
```

### 2. Purpose

- I received it from **[人]** as a **[场合]** gift.
- **[人]** gave it to me to show…
- I bought it mainly because I needed…

### 3. Description

- It is made of…；
- It comes in…；
- It is reasonably priced / good value for money；
- I mainly use it for **doing…**；
- One useful feature is…。

### 4. Feelings

- I find it extremely useful/convenient.
- Every time I use it, …
- It makes **[活动]** much easier.

### 5. Meaning

- It has sentimental value for me.
- It reminds me of…
- It is a symbol of…
- I would hate to lose it because…

### 物品完整示范

> An object that means a lot to me is a watch my father gave me when I graduated. It is a simple black watch with a metal frame, and it is not particularly expensive.
>
> I mainly use it to check the time during work because I do not always want to look at my phone. It is light, reliable and easy to wear, so from a practical point of view it is very useful.
>
> More importantly, it has sentimental value. My father gave it to me to congratulate me on completing an important stage of my education. Every time I wear it, it reminds me of that day and of the support my family gave me. That is why I would hate to lose it.

### 必背语块

- receive it as a…gift；
- mainly use it for doing；
- reasonably priced；
- sentimental value；
- remind me of；
- a symbol of。

## 十、Part 3 四大题型

## 1. Give and Support an Opinion

结构：

```text
立场 → 原因 → 例子 → 限制/结果
```

背诵句：

- I strongly believe that…
- From my perspective, …
- The main reason is that…
- A good example would be…
- This means that…

示例：

> I believe public parks are essential in large cities because they give residents space to exercise and relax. A good example is office workers who spend most of the day indoors. Having a nearby green space can improve both physical and mental wellbeing.

## 2. Agree or Disagree

背诵句：

- I completely agree that…
- I agree to a certain extent, but…
- I can see what you mean; however…
- I cannot fully agree because…

示例：

> I disagree that parents should make every important decision for their children. Parents have more experience and should offer advice, but young people also need to learn how to make choices and accept the consequences.

## 3. Advantages and Disadvantages

背诵句：

- One of the main benefits is that…
- Another advantage is…
- The main drawback is…
- On balance, …

示例：

> One major benefit of online shopping is convenience, because people can compare prices without travelling to several stores. The main drawback is that they cannot always check the quality or size before buying. On balance, it is useful, but not suitable for every product.

## 4. Problems and Solutions

背诵句：

- One major issue is…
- This is mainly due to…
- The authorities could…
- Individuals could also…
- In the long run, …

示例：

> One major issue is traffic congestion, which is mainly caused by heavy dependence on private cars. Local authorities could improve public transport and create safer cycling routes. Individuals could also avoid driving for short journeys.

## 5. Part 3 社会视角

从以下角度选择：

```text
individual / family / education / business
government / technology / environment
```

常见影响：

```text
cost / time / health / equality / convenience
productivity / relationships / privacy / environment
```

## 6. Part 3完整示范

题目：Do you think technology has improved communication?

> Overall, I think it has improved communication in terms of speed and convenience. People can remain in touch with family members who live in different cities or countries, and businesses can cooperate without being in the same office. However, technology does not automatically improve the quality of communication. If people rely too heavily on short messages, they may have fewer meaningful face-to-face conversations. So I would say it is beneficial, provided that it is used in a balanced way.

## 十一、时态、时间介词与高频语法

### 1. 六个重点时态

| 时态 | 主要用途 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| 一般现在 | 事实与习惯 | I work from home. |
| 现在进行 | 当前或临时状态 | I’m living with my parents at the moment. |
| 现在完成 | 经历或延续状态 | I’ve lived here for five years. |
| 一般过去 | 已完成事件 | I visited it last year. |
| 过去进行 | 故事背景 | I was studying when he called. |
| 一般将来 | 预测或决定 | I think it will improve. |

### 2. 过去时故事组合

> I was walking home when it started to rain. I had left my umbrella at work, so I went into a café and waited there until the rain stopped.

### 3. used to

- I used to be quite shy.
- I didn’t use to exercise regularly.
- Did you use to live there?

### 4. 条件句

- If I get the chance, I’ll visit Japan.
- If I had more free time, I’d learn photography.
- If I had known about the event, I would have gone.

### 5. 定语从句

- a person who helped me；
- a place which is close to my home；
- a city where public transport is reliable。

### 6. 高频错误

- He lives，不是 He live；
- I come from / I am from，不是 I am come from；
- some advice/information，不随意加 `s`；
- 过去故事不要中途全部变回现在时；
- `If I have a chance, I will…`，if 从句不使用 `will`。
