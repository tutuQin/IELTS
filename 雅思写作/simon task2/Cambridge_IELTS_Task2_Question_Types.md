## 📝 二、 各题型真题原题与中文对照清单

### 1. 观点题 (Opinion Essay) —— 共 17 篇
> **特点：** 题干给出一个核心观点或某种社会现象，要求考生表达支持或反对（Agree or Disagree），或者判断其是否属于积极/消极的发展（Positive or Negative Development）。

*   **剑4 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that university students should pay tuition fees because the education benefits the individual student rather than society as a whole. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为大学生应该支付学费，因为大学教育受益的是学生个人而不是整个社会。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？
*   **剑4 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Scientific research today attempts to go too far. There are certain things in the world which we simply should not try to understand. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 如今的科学研究尝试走得太远了。世界上有些事情我们根本不应该去试图理解。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？
*   **剑5 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Universities should accept equal numbers of male and female students in every subject. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 大学应该在每个专业招收相同数量的男生和女生。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？
*   **剑6 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "High sales of popular consumer goods reflect the power of advertising rather than the real needs of the society. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 畅销消费品的高销量反映的是广告的力量，而不是社会的真正需求。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？
*   **剑9 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programmes (for example working for a charity, improving the neighbourhood or teaching sports to younger children). To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为无偿社区服务应该成为高中课程的强制性组成部分（例如为慈善机构工作、改善社区或教小孩子体育）。你在多大程度上同意或不同意？
*   **剑9 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Every year several languages die out. Some people think that this is not important because life will be easier if there are fewer languages in the world. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this opinion?"
    *   **Zh:** 每年都有几种语言消失。有些人认为这并不重要，因为如果世界上的语言变少了，生活会变得更轻松。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个观点？
*   **剑10 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Countries are becoming more and more similar because people are able to buy the same products anywhere in the world. Do you think this is a positive or negative development?"
    *   **Zh:** 随着人们可以在世界任何地方买到相同的商品，国家之间变得越来越相似。你认为这是一个积极的还是消极的发展？
*   **剑11 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Governments should spend money on railways rather than roads. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?"
    *   **Zh:** 政府应该把钱花在铁路上，而不是公路上。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个声明？
*   **剑11 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people claim that not enough of the waste from homes is recycled. They say that the only way to increase recycling is for governments to make it a legal requirement. To what extent do you think laws are needed to make people recycle more of their waste?"
    *   **Zh:** 一些人声称来自家庭的垃圾没有得到足够的回收。他们说增加回收的唯一方法是政府将其作为法律要求。你在多大程度上认为需要制定法律来让人们回收更多的垃圾？
*   **剑13 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Living in a country where you have to speak a foreign language can cause serious social problems, as well as practical problems. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?"
    *   **Zh:** 生活在一个必须说外语的国家会引起严重的社会问题以及实际生活问题。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个声明？
*   **剑13 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say it is important to keep your home and your workplace tidy, with everything organised and in the correct place. What is your opinion about this?"
    *   **Zh:** 有人说保持家里和工作场所整洁、一切井井有条并放在正确的位置是很重要的。你对此有什么看法？
*   **剑14 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say that music is a good way of bringing people of different cultures and ages together. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this opinion?"
    *   **Zh:** 有人说音乐是拉近不同文化和年龄人群距离的好方法。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个观点？
*   **剑15 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "In the future, nobody will buy printed books or newspapers because they will be able to read everything they want online without paying. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?"
    *   **Zh:** 未来没有人会购买纸质书或报纸，因为他们将能够在网上免费阅读他们想看的一切。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个声明？
*   **剑16 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Many manufactured food and drink products contain high levels of sugar, which causes many health problems. Sugary products should be made more expensive to encourage people to consume less sugar. Do you agree or disagree?"
    *   **Zh:** 许多加工食品和饮料含有高糖，这引起了许多健康问题。含糖产品应该卖得更贵，以鼓励人们减少糖分摄入。你同意还是不同意？
*   **剑17 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Nowadays, a growing number of people with health problems are trying alternative medicines and treatments instead of visiting their usual doctor. Do you think this is a positive or a negative development?"
    *   **Zh:** 如今，越来越多有健康问题的人开始尝试替代药物和疗法，而不是去看常规医生。你认为这是一个积极的还是消极的发展？
*   **剑18 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "The most important aim of science should be to improve people's lives. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this statement?"
    *   **Zh:** 科学最重要的目标应当是改善人们的生活。你在多大程度上同意或不同意这个声明？
*   **剑18 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "In many countries around the world, rural people are moving to cities, so the population in the countryside is decreasing. Do you think this is a positive or a negative development?"
    *   **Zh:** 在世界上许多国家，农村人口正在流向城市，因此农村人口正在减少。你认为这是一个积极的还是消极的发展？

### 2. 双方讨论题 (Discussion Essay) —— 共 23 篇
> **特点：** 题干给出两个相反或不同的社会观点，要求考生讨论双方的论据（Discuss both views），并给出自己的个人立场观点（Give your opinion）。

*   **剑5 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people think that a sense of competition in children should be encouraged. Others believe that children who are taught to co-operate rather than compete become more useful adults. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为应该鼓励孩子的竞争意识。另一些人则认为，被教导合作而非竞争的孩子会长成更有用的成年人。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑5 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Research indicates that the characteristics we are born with have much more influence on our personality and development than any experiences we may have in our life. Which do you consider to be the major influence? Discuss both views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 研究表明，我们天生的特质对我们个性和发展的影响，远大于我们一生的任何经历。你认为哪一个是主要影响？讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑6 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Successful sports professionals can earn a great deal more money than people in other important professions. Some people think this is fully justified while others think it is unfair. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 成功的体育明星赚的钱比其他重要行业的人多得多。一些人认为这完全合理，而另一些人认为这不公平。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑6 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that visitors to other countries should follow local customs and behaviour. Others disagree and think that the host country should welcome cultural differences. Discuss both these views and give your opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为去其他国家旅游的游客应该遵循当地的习俗和行为。另一些人不同意，认为东道国应该欢迎文化差异。讨论双方观点并给出你的看法。
*   **剑6 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Some people prefer to spend their lives doing the same things and avoiding change. Others, however, think that change is always a good thing. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人宁愿一生做同样的事情并避免改变。然而，另一些人认为改变总是好事。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑7 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "It is generally believed that some people are born with certain talents, for instance for sport or music, and others are not. However, it is sometimes claimed that any child can be taught to become a good sports person or musician. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 人们普遍认为有些人天生就有某些才能，例如运动或音乐，而其他人没有。然而，有时也有人声称，任何孩子都可以通过教学成为出色的运动员或音乐家。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑7 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that there should be fixed punishments for each type of crime. Others, however, argue that the circumstances of an individual crime, and the motivation for committing it, should always be taken into account when deciding on the punishment. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为每种犯罪都应该有固定的刑罚。然而，另起一些人认为在决定惩罚时，应始终考虑个人犯罪的具体情况和犯罪动机。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑7 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Some people think that universities should provide graduates with the knowledge and skills needed in the workplace. Others think that the true function of a university should be to give access to knowledge for its own sake, regardless of whether the course is useful to an employer. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为大学应该为毕业生提供职场所需的知识和技能。另一些人则认为，大学的真正功能应该是为获取知识本身提供途径，而不管这门课程对雇主是否有用。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑8 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Some people think that universities/schools should teach children to be good members of society. Others believe that parents should teach children this. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为学校应该教育孩子如何成为良好的社会成员。另一些人认为父母应该教孩子这些。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑9 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say that the best way to improve public health is by increasing the number of sports facilities. Others, however, say that this would have little effect on public health and that other measures are required. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人说改善公共卫生的最好方法是增加体育设施的数量。然而，另一些人说这对公共卫生的影响微乎其微，还需要其他措施。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑10 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people think that all university students should study whatever they like. Others believe that they should only be allowed to study subjects that will be useful in the future, such as those related to science and technology. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为所有的大学生都应该学习他们喜欢的任何东西。另一些人认为他们应该只被允许学习未来有用的科目，比如与科学和技术相关的科目。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑11 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say that the only reason for learning a foreign language is in order to travel to or work in a foreign country. Others say that these are not the only reasons why someone should learn a foreign language. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人说学习外语的唯一原因是为了去外国旅行或在外国工作。另一些人说，这些并不是人们学习外语的唯一原因。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑11 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Many governments think that economic progress is their most important goal. Some people, however, think that other types of progress are equally important for a country. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 许多政府认为经济增长是他们最重要的目标。然而，另一些人认为，其他类型的发展对一个国家来说同样重要。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑12 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that sharing information may be beneficial for every sector. But in some cases, it may create a dangerous situation. Discuss both sides and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为共享信息对每个行业都是有益的。但在某些情况下，它可能会造成危险的局面。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑12 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that allowing children to make their own choices on everyday matters (such as food, clothes and entertainment) is likely to result in a society of individuals who only think about their own wishes. Other people believe that it is important for children to make decisions about matters that affect them. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为，允许孩子在日常事务上（如食物、衣服和娱乐）做出自己的选择，可能会导致社会中的每个人都只考虑自己的愿望。另一些人则认为，让孩子对影响他们的事情做出决定是很重要的。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑12 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "In a number of countries, some people think it is necessary to spend large sums of money on constructing new railway lines for very fast trains between cities. Others believe the money should be spent on improving existing public transport. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 在一些国家，有人认为有必要花大笔资金在城市之间修建新的高速铁路线。另一些人认为，这些钱应该花在改善现有的公共交通上。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑13 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people think that advertising on TV is useful for viewers, while others disagree. Discuss both sides and give your opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为电视上的广告对观众是有用的，而另一些人则不同意。讨论双方观点并给出你的看法。
*   **剑13 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say history is one of the most important school subjects. Other people think that, in today's world, subjects like science and technology are more important than history. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人说历史是学校里最重要的科目之一。另一些人认为在当今世界，像科学和技术这样的科目比历史更重要。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑14 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that it is best to accept a bad situation, such as an unsatisfactory job or shortage of money. Others argue that it is better to try and improve such situations. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为最好接受糟糕的现状，比如一份不满意的工作或资金短缺。另一些人认为，最好尝试去改善这种情况。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑14 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say that the main environmental problem of our time is the loss of particular species of plants and animals. Others think that there are more important environmental problems. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人说我们时代最主要的空气和环境问题是某些特定动植物物种的灭绝。另一些人认为还有其他更重要的环境问题。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑15 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people say that advertising is extremely successful at persuading us to buy things. Other people think that advertising is so common that we no longer pay attention to it. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人说广告极大地成功说服我们购买东西。另一些人认为广告太普遍了，以至于我们不再关注它。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑17 Test 3:**
    *   **En:** "Some people believe that professionals, such as doctors and engineers, should be required to work in the country where they did their training. Others believe they should be free to work in another country if they wish. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些人认为像医生和工程师这样的专业人员应该被要求在他们接受培训的国家工作。另一些人认为，如果他们愿意，应该可以自由地去另一个国家工作。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。
*   **剑18 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Some university students want to learn about other subjects in addition to their main subjects. Others believe it is more important to give all their time and attention to studying for a qualification. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."
    *   **Zh:** 一些大学生想在专业课程之外学习其他学科。另一些人认为，把所有的时间和注意力都花在学习学位资格上更为重要。讨论双方观点并给出你自己的看法。

### 3. 利弊题 (Advantages & Disadvantages) —— 共 7 篇
> **特点：** 提问方式通常为 "Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"（利是否大于弊）或者 "What are the advantages and disadvantages...?"（列举利弊）。

*   **剑5 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "In some countries, young people are encouraged to work or travel for a year between finishing high school and starting university studies. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages for young people who decide to do this."
    *   **Zh:** 在一些国家，鼓励年轻人在中学毕业和开始大学学业之间工作或旅行一年。讨论决定这样做的年轻人的优势和劣势。
*   **剑9 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "Some experts believe that it is better for children to begin learning a foreign language at primary school rather than secondary school. Do the advantages of this outweigh the disadvantages?"
    *   **Zh:** 一些专家认为，儿童在小学而不是中学开始学习外语会更好。你认为这种做法利大于弊吗？
*   **剑10 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "Many museums charge for admission while others are free. Do you think the advantages of charging people for admission to museums outweigh the disadvantages?"
    *   **Zh:** 许多博物馆收取门票，而其他博物馆是免费的。你认为向人们收取博物馆门票费用的做法是利大于弊吗？
*   **剑15 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "In some cultures, children are often told that they can achieve anything if they try hard enough. What are the advantages and disadvantages of giving children this message?"
    *   **Zh:** 在某些文化中，孩子们经常被告知如果他们足够努力，他们就能取得任何成就。给孩子传递这种信息有什么好处和坏处？
*   **剑16 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "In the future, all cars, buses and trucks will be driverless. The only people travelling inside these vehicles will be passengers. Do you think the advantages of driverless vehicles outweigh the disadvantages?"
    *   **Zh:** 未来所有的轿车、公共汽车和卡车都将是无人驾驶的。这些车辆内的所有人将都只是乘客。你认为无人驾驶车辆的优势大于劣势吗？
*   **剑17 Test 1:**
    *   **En:** "It is important for people to take risks, both in their professional lives and their personal lives. Do you think the advantages of taking risks outweigh the disadvantages?"
    *   **Zh:** 对人们来说，在职业和个人生活中承担风险都是很重要的。你认为承担风险的优势大于劣势吗？
*   **剑18 Test 4:**
    *   **En:** "In many countries, people are now living longer than ever before. Some people say an ageing population creates problems for governments. Other people think there are benefits if society has more elderly people. To what extent do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"
    *   **Zh:** 在许多国家，人们现在的寿命比以往任何时候都长。有些人说人口老龄化给政府带来了问题。另一些人认为如果社会有更多的老年人会有好处。在多大程度上利大于弊？

### 4. 双问题/混合题 (Two-part / Mixed) —— 共 11 篇
> **特点：** 题干中包含两个独立的问题（通常在结尾有两个问号），考生必须在两个主体段中分别完整地回答这两个问题，缺一不可。

*   **剑4 Test 2:**
    *   **En:** "Happiness is considered very important in life. Why is it difficult to define? What factors are important in achieving happiness?"

## 💡 三、 备考建议与高频题型考向洞察

1.  **“双方讨论题 (Discussion)” 考频高居首位 (23/60)：**
    *   这表明大作文中最核心、考查最频繁的能力是**“客观辩证地对比两种不同立场，并给出一个不偏不倚的清晰个人观点”**。必须重点攻克双方讨论的四段式模板。
2.  **“观点题 (Opinion)” 依然是基石 (17/60)：**
    *   近年观点题的提问方式除了传统的 `Agree or Disagree` 之外，`Positive or Negative Development`（积极/消极发展）出镜率显著升高（如剑17、剑18）。这类题型本质上等同于同意/不同意，写单边或者平衡性双边皆可，但立场必须明确。
3.  **“双问题题型 (Two-part)” 不容小觑 (11/60)：**
    *   许多考生以为混合题是偏题，但实际上它的出题频率甚至超过了传统的利弊题和问题解决题。写双问题题时，切记**结构必须平衡**：用 Body 1 回答问题一，Body 2 回答问题二，千万不要把两问混在一起写，否则会严重扣除“连贯与衔接 (Coherence & Cohesion)”的分数。
4.  **“纯问题解决型 (Problem & Solution)” 极少单独出现 (2/60)：**
    *   传统的 `Causes & Solutions` 题在真题集里只有 2 篇（剑4 Test4、剑8 Test4）。现在的雅思更倾向于将“原因分析”与“观点表态/利弊评估”结合起来考查（即转化为**双问题/混合题**）。因此，考生不用把大量时间花在死背纯问题解决模板上，而应该熟练掌握如何灵活应对双重问题。
