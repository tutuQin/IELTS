# 👑 第一部分：雅思小作文万能底层铁律（全题型通用）

## 1. 黄金四段论结构（万能骨架）

不论面对什么图表，考场上永远只写四段：

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。彻底改写题目（Paraphrase）。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。归纳 2 个最宏观的核心特征，**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3-4句话。对第一组数据/图表/步骤进行合乎逻辑的对比汇报。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。对第二组数据/图表/步骤进行合乎逻辑的对比汇报。

## 2. 核心核心：主语与数字的完美搭配

小作文丢分的核心原因，是句子中“主语”和“数字”张冠李戴。请死记以下两条万能公式：

* **公式 A（以“物/人”为主语）**：`[主体/人] + 动词 + 数字`
* *例句：* **The UK** consumed **40,000 tonnes** of gas.


* **公式 B（以“数字/比例/数量”为主语）**：`The number/percentage/proportion of + [主体/人] + was + 数字`
* *例句：* **The proportion of women** taking language classes was **42%**.

# 👑 第二部分：6大核心题型 + 地图变体（背诵模版与九分范文）

## 题型 1：动态线图/柱图（Line Graph / Dynamic Bar）

* **底层逻辑**：考查“时间轴上的趋势演变”。核心是描述上升、下降、波动、持平和交叉点。

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。彻底改写题目（Paraphrase）。将 `shows` 换成 `compares`，将具体年份改写为 `over a period of X years`。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。写最突出的两个趋势：谁始终最高/谁增长最快，谁始终最低/谁下降最猛。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 2-3句话。比较特定时间点（通常是起点）的各项数据，并描述紧随其后的**核心交叉点/超越点**。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。比较随后的动态发展趋势、峰值或稳定期，并以终点数据收尾。
* **分组方法**：
  * 方法一 **按数值高低分组**（最推荐）：巨头项目写 P3，中下游项目打包写 P4。
  * 方法二 **按趋势异同分组**：上升的归一组，下降/平稳的归另一组。
  * 方法三 **按时间跨度平分**：前半段年份写 P3，后半段年份写 P4。

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The [线图/柱图] **compares the entries for** [主体] **in terms of their** [度量标准] **over a period of** [总年数] **years from** [起始年] **to** [截止年].
* **Overview**: **It is clear that** [主体A] **showed an upward trend, whereas the figures for** [主体B] **decreased significantly over the period shown. Additionally,** [主体A] **remained the most popular / highest category throughout.**
* **Details 1 (写上升/高位)**: **In** [起始年], **the number of** [主体A] **stood at** [数字]. **This figure then saw a steady rise over the following** [X] **years, peaking at** [数字] **in** [某年], **before dropping slightly to** [数字] **at the end of the period.**
* **Details 2 (写下降/低位)**: **By contrast, the proportion of** [主体B] **started at a much higher level, at** [数字] **in** [起始年]. **However, it experienced a dramatic decline to** [数字] **in** [某年], **and subsequently remained stable at approximately** [数字].

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（英国能源消耗变化）

> The line graph compares the amount of energy consumed in the UK from three different fuel sources over a period of 30 years from 1980 to 2010.
> It is clear that oil remained the main source of energy in the UK throughout the 30-year period. While the consumption of petroleum and natural gas showed an upward trend, coal usage decreased significantly.
> In 1980, energy generated from oil stood at approximately 35 million tonnes, which was well over twice as high as the figure for natural gas. Oil consumption then saw a steady rise over the following two decades, peaking at nearly 42 million tonnes in 2000, before dropping slightly to 38 million tonnes at the end of the period.
> By contrast, coal started at a much higher level than natural gas, at 16 million tonnes in 1980. However, coal consumption experienced a dramatic decline to only 6 million tonnes in 2010. During the same period, natural gas usage saw a rapid increase, overtaking coal in 1990 and reaching 14 million tonnes by 2010.

## 题型 2：静态柱图/表格（Static Bar / Table）

* **底层逻辑**：**没有时间变化**！不要写上升下降！核心考查“分类之间的大小比较与倍数关系”。

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，交代图表类型、比较对象、分类标准和具体年份。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。指出谁占比/数值最高，谁最低。若有两个指标（如表格的“游客数”和“收入”），可各抓一个极值。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 2-3句话。聚焦数据最大的分类/指标，用倍数关系（`twice as high as`）、排名（`followed by`）和合并同类项（`each`）进行高效对比。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3句话。描述剩余分类/第二个指标。用区间法（`between X and Y`）打包不重要的中下游数据，用 `a mere` 点出最小值收尾。
* **分组方法**：
  * **表格题按列/指标分组**：P3 纯写指标A（如游客数量），P4 纯写指标B（如旅游收入）。
  * **静态柱图按数值高低分组**：最大项 vs 最小项，或用比较级 `higher than` / `lower than` 进行横向对比。
  * 面对 20+ 个数字，只挑 10 个核心数据（最高、最低、异常、极端）。

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The [柱图/表格] **illustrates the amount/percentage of** [主体] **in** [地点] **in** [具体年份], **broken down by** [分类标准].
* **Overview**: **Overall, it is noticeable that** [主体A] **accounted for the highest proportion of** [项1], **while** [主体B] **was the least popular choice. Furthermore,** [地点/分类] **recorded the largest figures in almost all categories.**
* **Details 1 (写最大/极值)**: **Looking at the figures for** [大分类], [主体A] **was by far the most dominant, with a total of** [数字], **which was well over twice as high as the figure for** [主体B] (**at only** [数字]).
* **Details 2 (写次大/微弱群体)**: **In terms of the other categories, the numbers of** [主体C] **and** [主体D] **were relatively similar, at** [数字] **and** [数字] **respectively. By contrast, a mere** [数字/百分比] **of people chose** [主体E], **making it the lowest recorded group.**

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（六国消费比例项目）

> The table illustrates the percentage of national consumer expenditure on three categories of items in five countries in 2002.
> Overall, it is noticeable that consumers in all five countries spent the largest proportion of their income on food, drinks and tobacco. Furthermore, Turkey recorded the highest expenditure in almost all categories.
> Looking at the figures for food, drinks and tobacco, Turkey was by far the most dominant, with a total of 32.14% of expenditure, which was well over twice as high as the figure for the UK, at only 15.77%. Ireland also recorded a high percentage in this category, at nearly 29%.
> In terms of the other two categories, the percentages spent on clothing and footwear were relatively similar across all nations, ranging from roughly 5% to 6.5%. By contrast, a mere 1.98% of Swedish income was spent on leisure and education, making it the lowest recorded group in the table.

## 题型 3：饼图（Pie Chart）

* **底层逻辑**：核心展示“份额、整体与局部的占比关系”。极度考查百分比、分数的互换表达。

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，交代饼图数量、对比的分类、国家/地点和年份。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。第一句抓两国/两年的**共同趋势**（如“某类别都下降了”），第二句抓**最大差异**（如“A国在娱乐上远高于B国”）。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3句话。集中对比 Overview 中提到的核心矛盾类别（如占比最大的两个项目），跨国家、跨年份交叉对比。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。描述剩余的次要或新兴类别，最后一句用 `Finally` 把最不重要的“其他”一笔带过。
* **分组方法**：**按类别属性分组**（绝不按国家或年份分开写！）：核心矛盾类别（如饮食+能源）写 P3，次要类别（如休闲+交通+其他）打包写 P4。
* **时态陷阱**：用 `in 2008` 搭配一般过去时；用 `by 2008` 搭配过去完成时 `had fallen`（考官超爱的语法加分点）。

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The pie charts **compare the proportion of** [总体] **allocated to** [分类] **in two different years,** [年份1] **and** [年份2].
* **Overview**: **It is clear that** [类别A] **represented the largest segment of the charts in both years. Meanwhile, the most significant change occurred in the percentage of** [类别B], **which grew dramatically.**
* **Details 1**: **In the first year,** [类别A] **constituted the majority of the total, accounting for precisely** [百分比]. **By the final year, this figure fell slightly to** [分数, 如 a third], **but it still remained the dominant choice among all groups.**
* **Details 2**: **In contrast, younger age groups or small sectors like** [类别B] **were initially in the minority, at a mere** [百分比]. **However, over the next years, this sector expanded rapidly, doubling its share to reach** [百分比] **by** [最终年].

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（某智能手机市场份额变化）

> The pie charts compare the market share of five mobile phone operating systems in two different years, 2011 and 2013.
> It is clear that Android represented the largest segment of the market in both years. Meanwhile, the most significant change occurred in the percentage of Symbian users, which dropped dramatically.
> In 2011, Android constituted the largest share of the total market, accounting for precisely 38.5%. By 2013, this figure grew significantly to over two-thirds of the total market (67.4%), cementing its position as the dominant operating system.
> In contrast, Symbian, which was initially in the second position at 22.1% in 2011, experienced a severe decline. By 2013, Symbian users were in the minority, representing a mere 2.3% of the total. The remaining operating systems, including iOS and Windows, remained relatively stable, each making up between 5% and 15% across both years.

## 题型 4：双图组合图（2 Different Charts）

* **底层逻辑**：通常由“柱图+饼图”或“线图+表格”组成。**两图没有直接因果关系，各自独立客观汇报，绝不跨图硬连！**

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。用 `and` 将两个图表各自的主题串联成一个高级长难句。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。第一句概括图1最显眼的特征（如“女性远多于男性”），第二句概括图2最显眼的特征（如“40岁以上人群占主体”）。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3句话。**只写图1**。用标志词 `According to the bar chart` 划定范围，依次对比最受欢迎、次受欢迎和最不受欢迎的分类。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。**只写图2**。用标志词 `Looking at the pie chart` 开头，对比多数群体（Majority）与少数群体（Minority）。
* **分组方法**：**各司其职，各占一段**：P3 = 图1全部数据，P4 = 图2全部数据。图表没有给出的关联，**绝对不要自己发明因果关系**。

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The bar chart **compares the numbers of** [图1主题], **and the pie chart shows the** [图2主题].
* **Overview**: **It is clear that significantly more** [主体A] **than** [主体B] **attended/used** [空间] **overall. In terms of** [图2特征], **the vast majority of people were** [图2最大特征].
* **Details 1 (纯写图1)**: **According to the bar chart,** [分类A] **had the highest number of participants overall, with** [数据1] **and** [数据2]. **By contrast,** [分类B] **was the least popular, and it was the only category where** [特殊反常交叉点, 如男性超越女性].
* **Details 2 (纯写图2)**: **Looking at the** [图2类型] **chart, we can see that** [类别X] **represented the largest group, accounting for** [百分比] **of the total. To be precise, older/younger users made up over [分数, 如 two-thirds] of the total participants.**

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（晚间课程男女及年龄分布图）

> The bar chart compares the numbers of males and females who took four different evening classes at an adult education centre in 2009, and the pie chart shows the age profile of these attendees.
> It is clear that significantly more women than men attended evening classes overall. In terms of age, the vast majority of people who took courses were aged 40 or over.
> According to the bar chart, language classes had the highest number of participants overall, with 40 female and 20 male students. Painting was also a popular choice among both genders, attracting 30 female attendees and only slightly fewer men, at 25. By contrast, sculpture was the least popular course, and it was the only class where male students outnumbered females, with 15 men and around 10 women.
> Looking at the age profile pie chart, we can see that people aged 50 or over represented the largest group of attendees, accounting for 42% of the total. The second largest group was those aged 40 to 49, at 26%. To be precise, older students made up over two-thirds of the participants. In contrast, the younger age groups were in the minority, with 20-to-29-year-olds making up 11% of the total, and under-20s representing a mere 5%.

## 题型 5：动态生产/回收流程图（Process Diagram）

* **底层逻辑**：工业制造或循环回收。**没数字、没趋势，核心考查"步骤衔接词"与"70%统治全篇的被动语态（is/are + 过去分词）"**。

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，将 `shows` 换成 `illustrates the stages involved in the [production/recycling] of...`。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。第一句明确总步骤数（`there are X main stages`），第二句交代起点与终点（`beginning with... and ending with...`）。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3-4句话。顺次描写流程的**前半部分**步骤（如收集→分类→清洗→粉碎），每步之间用不同的衔接词连接。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。顺次描写流程的**后半部分**步骤（如熔化→塑形→包装→最终产品），以 `Finally` 收尾。
* **分组方法**：**将总步骤一分为二**：前半段工艺写 P3，后半段成品制作写 P4。若为双流程图（如制造+回收），P3 写制造流程，P4 写回收流程。
* **语法核心**：70%-80% 谓语动词使用被动语态（`is/are + 过去分词`）。衔接词不能重复：`At the first stage` → `Following this` → `Next` → `Finally`。

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The flow diagram **illustrates the stages involved in the** [生产/回收核心话题, 如 production of cement].
* **Overview**: **It is clear that there are** [数字] **main stages in the process, beginning with the** [步骤1名词化] **and ending with the** [最终步骤名词化].
* **Details 1 (前半段)**: **At the first stage of the process,** [原材料] **is/are** [过去分词] **into a machine. Following this, the** [半成品] **is/are transported to** [地点], **where they are** [过去分词] **according to** [标准].
* **Details 2 (后半段)**: **Once** [过去分词, 如 sorted/cleaned], **the material is passed into a furnace to be heated at high temperatures. At the final stage, the resulting** [形容词化主体, 如 molten/ground] **material can be shaped or moulded to create the useful end products.**

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（玻璃瓶制造与回收双图）

> The first flow diagram illustrates the process of glass container production, and the second diagram shows the steps in the process of recycling used glass.
> It is clear that glass is made using three main raw materials, and the manufacturing process consists of four distinct stages. By contrast, it requires five steps to turn used glass back into new glass products.
> At the first stage of the production process, sand, soda ash, and limestone are mixed together. Next, this mixture is heated in a high-temperature furnace at approximately 1500°C until it melts. At the following stage, the liquid glass can be shaped or blown into various containers, which are then ready to be used as end products.
> The recycling process begins with the collection of waste glass bottles by consumers. Following this, the collected glass is transported to a plant where it is sorted according to color and washed to remove impurities. At the fourth stage, the clean glass is crushed and melted in a recycling furnace. Finally, the resulting molten glass can be moulded to create new bottles and jars, turning used glass back into useful products.

## 题型 6：提议改造方案图（Comparison Diagram）

* **底层逻辑**：对比现有设计与未来的重新设计提议方案。**核心考查"现有一般现在时（has/contains）"与"提议一般将来时 will 的被动语态（will be built）"的丝滑交织**。

### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，将 `existing plan` 换成 `current layout`，将 `proposed plan` 换成 `plan to redesign`。
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。第一句写最大变化（如"拆除中央大厅，创造开放式空间"），第二句写保持不变的部分（如"外墙和正门位置不变"）。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3句话。每句内部都是"现有 vs 未来"的交织对比。先用**一般现在时**描述现状（`leads into`, `contains`），再用**将来被动语态**描述改造（`will disappear`, `will be removed`）。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。描述其余的改造细节（新设施安装、门的替换等），最后一句用 `once the construction project has been done` 收尾。
* **分组方法**：**按物理空间分组**：P3 聚焦核心区域（如中央大厅和客厅），P4 聚焦次要区域（如厨房和楼梯）。**绝不写成**"P3 纯写现有图、P4 纯写未来图"（考官眼里这叫割裂，缺乏对比）。
* **三大时态组合**：
  * 过去 vs 过去 → 全用一般过去时
  * 过去 vs 现在 → 过去时 + 现在时/现在完成时
  * 现在 vs 未来 → 现在时 + will 被动语态

### 📋 通用高分模版

* **Introduction**: The two plans **compare the current layout of** [建筑名称] **with a proposed plan to redesign this living space.**
* **Overview**: **It is clear that the main translation involves the removal/restructuring of** [变化最大区] **to create a larger** [新空间]. **By contrast, the exterior walls and the position of the main entrance door will remain completely unchanged.**
* **Details 1**: **At present, the entrance leads into a** [旧设施], **which contains** [特征]. **According to the design proposal, this area will disappear as the internal walls are to be removed. This alteration will achieve a much larger, continuous living space.**
* **Details 2**: **Looking at the other modifications, the plan to redesign involves installing a new set of** [新设施, 如 winding stairs] **in the corner. Furthermore, the single door will be replaced by double doors. Finally, the project will be completed once the construction work has been done.**

### 📝 Band 9 官方范文（一楼房屋改建提议方案）

> The two plans compare the current layout of the ground floor of a house with a proposed plan to redesign this living space.
> It is clear that the main translation involves the removal of the central hall to create a larger, open-plan living area. By contrast, the exterior walls and the position of the main entrance door will remain completely unchanged.
> At present, the main entrance door leads into a central hall, which contains a straight staircase and doors opening into the kitchen and living room. According to the design proposal, this hall will disappear as the internal walls and doors are to be removed. This alteration will achieve a much larger, continuous living space, and the old stairs will be replaced by a new staircase.
> Looking at the other modifications, the plan to redesign involves installing a new set of winding stairs in the corner of the room, which will also include an integrated under-stair storage cupboard. Furthermore, the single door currently connecting the hall and the kitchen will be replaced by double doors leading directly from the expanded living area. Finally, the installation of brand new kitchen furniture and appliances will be achieved once the construction project has been done.

## 💡 终极彩蛋：地图题（Map Questions）无缝降维转换法则

Simon 老师在第 9 课最后强调，遇到地图题千万别慌，它们只是以下两款图表的变体：

### 变体 A："过去 vs 现在/未来"的双地图对比题

* **等同于 $\rightarrow$ 【题型 6：提议改造方案图】**。

#### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，如 `The two maps compare the layout of [地点] before and after the development of [设施].`
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。第一句用 `underwent a complete transformation` 抓总变动，第二句用 `remained completely unchanged` 锁死不变的自然特征（如海岸线、沙滩）。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3-4句话。描述改建前的原始状态（用一般过去时），然后交织描述核心区域的改建（新建了什么、拆除了什么）。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。描述剩余区域的改建与保留不变的自然特征。
* **分组方法**：按物理空间/方位分组，一半写"变"，一半写"不变"。
* *万能句型背诵：* `The old building was demolished to make way for a new car park.`（旧建筑被拆除，为新停车场让路）。

### 变体 B："多阶段演变历史地图"

* **等同于 $\rightarrow$ 【题型 5：流程图】**。

#### 🧠 写作思路（四段论拆解）

* **Paragraph 1: Introduction（引言段）** —— 1句话。改写题目，如 `The three maps illustrate the stages of development of [地点] over a period from [起始年] to [终止年].`
* **Paragraph 2: Overview（概述段）** —— 2句话。概括总共有几个发展阶段，以及最显著的空间扩张方向。**绝对不带任何具体数字数据**！
* **Paragraph 3: Details 1（细节描述段 1）** —— 3-4句话。按时间顺序描写前 1-2 个阶段的城市面貌与扩张特征。
* **Paragraph 4: Details 2（细节描述段 2）** —— 3-4句话。按时间顺序描写最后阶段的演变，强调最终状态。
* **分组方法**：按时间阶段顺次描写，像流程图一样使用步骤衔接词（`At the first stage of development` → `Following this` → `By the final stage`）。
* *核心词汇背诵：* `The city grew` / `The residential area expanded northward` / `The forest disappeared`.

# 👑 第三部分：考官高分核心词汇接力库（提分全靠它们！）

每天清晨朗读背诵这些短语，直接平替平庸词汇：

* **平替 only（仅仅）** $\rightarrow$ `a mere 5%` / `a mere 15 people`
* **平替 change（彻底改变）** $\rightarrow$ `underwent a complete transformation`
* **平替 more than（数量上超越）** $\rightarrow$ `male students outnumbered females`
* **平替 build（新建建筑）** $\rightarrow$ `was erected` / `was constructed`
* **平替 turn into（改造空间）** $\rightarrow$ `was converted into a multi-media room`
* **平替 same（保持不变）** $\rightarrow$ `remained completely unchanged`
* **高分数据挂靠后缀（独立主格）** $\rightarrow$ `..., attracting 30 female attendees.` / `..., with 40 female and 20 male students.`
