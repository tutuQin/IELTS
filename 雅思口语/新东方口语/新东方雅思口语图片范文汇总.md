## Topic 1: Describe a park or a garden in your city (公园与花园)

**话题核心**：地点类 - 公园/自然景区 (Red Hill Park 红山公园)
**推荐适用话题**：
- An indoor or outdoor place where you can study
- A building you like
- A quiet place you found
- A place in your city you like to visit with your friends
- A public place needs to be improved
- A leisure facility you would like to have in your hometown

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    There are many beautiful tourist attractions in my country, especially in my hometown, Xinjiang, but I would like to talk about the Red Hill Park today. The Red Hill Park lies in the center of the city, it's quite close to some office buildings and a stadium.

    It's green everywhere in summer, there are a lot of trees and endless grass field. And also a lot of streams flowing across the park. Besides, you can find a tower when you climb onto the top of the hill, which is the landmark of our city, even the local TV stations use its image as their logo.

    Ever since I was a little kid, my family and I would go to the Red Hill Park in both summers and winters. We always climb the hill and row the boat to set ourselves free from the pressure of our daily lives. And when we get tired, having a picnic would be a nice choice. On the other hand, it's a paradise for people to go ice skating in winters. It's like a magnet attracting millions of people flooding here for enjoying the outdoor activities in the freezing seasons.

    And in my point of view, apart from all the unforgettable views and all the joyful activities, the main reason that I like to go to the Red Hill Park in the holidays is that it's not very far from where I live. It only takes me 15 minutes to walk there. In addition, as a student, I'm not that loaded financially, and a Red Hill Park trip isn't going to cost too much, I guess that's one of the reasons that I love to go there too.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    我国有很多美丽的旅游景点，特别是在我的家乡新疆，但今天我想谈谈红山公园。红山公园位于市中心，离一些写字楼和一个体育场非常近。

    夏天的时候，这里到处都是一片绿意盎然，有许多树木和无边无际的草地。公园里还有很多小溪流过。此外，当你爬到山顶时，你会看到一座塔，那是我们城市的标志性建筑，甚至当地电视台都用它的形象作为台标。

    从我很小的时候起，我和我的家人就会在夏天和冬天去红山公园。我们总是通过爬山和划船来让我们从日常生活的压力中解脱出来。当我们累了的时候，野餐会是一个不错的选择。另一方面，在冬天，这里是人们滑冰的天堂。它就像一块磁铁，吸引着成千上万的人在寒冷的季节涌入这里享受户外活动。

    在我看来，除了所有令人难忘的风景和所有欢乐的活动外，我喜欢在假期去红山公园的主要原因是因为它离我住的地方不远。我只需要走15分钟就能到那里。此外，作为一名学生，我的经济并不富裕，去一次红山公园不会花费太多，我想这也是我喜欢去那里的原因之一。

## Topic 2: Describe a country or city you want to visit/live/work in (向往的城市)

**话题核心**：地点类 - 城市 (Los Angeles 洛杉矶)
**推荐适用话题**：
- A beautiful city you visited
- An interesting part of your country
- A place you would like to travel that is far away from your home
- A country or city where you want to live or work in the future

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    If there is one city that I would love to visit in the future, it must be Los Angeles. Actually I know an interesting fact about it, the name of that city is not English but Spanish which means "those angels." That's why Los Angeles is also known as "the city of angels," and there are a great number of Latins living there nowadays.

    Los Angeles is located in the west coast of America and it is the major city of the state of California. I have never been to the city, but I saw some pictures of the beautiful beaches and palm trees which really attracted me a lot. A few friends of mine are living in Los Angeles, they often send me some pictures of the attractions in the city, it makes me want to be there more eagerly.

    The main reason that I want to visit Los Angeles is that I'm a big fan of Kobe Bryant. I wish I could have a chance to see him play but it never happened. Now that he has already retired, I still have this dream to go to the Staples Center and watch a live game of Los Angeles Lakers. I think that is going to be one of the happiest moments in my life.

    Well, I hope that someday the opportunity would come and I would be ready for a trip to my dream city!

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    如果未来有一座我想去旅游的城市，那一定是洛杉矶。其实我知道一个关于它的趣闻，这座城市的名字不是英语，而是西班牙语，意思是‘那些天使’。这就是为什么洛杉矶也被称为‘天使之城’，如今那里居住着大量的拉丁裔。

    洛杉矶位于美国西海岸，是加利福尼亚州的主要城市。我虽然从未去过这座城市，但我看到过一些美丽海滩和棕榈树的照片，这真的很吸引我。我的几个朋友住在洛杉矶，他们经常给我发一些城市景点的照片，这让我更渴望去那里。

    我想去洛杉矶的主要原因是因为我是科比·布莱恩特的超级粉丝。我真希望自己能有机会看他打球，但这从未发生过。既然他已经退役了，我依然有这个梦想去斯台普斯中心，观看一场洛杉矶湖人队的现场比赛。我想那将是我人生中最幸福的时刻之一。

    好吧，我希望有一天机会能够来临，而我已经准备好前往我梦想的城市旅行！

## Topic 3: Describe a happy event / celebration (令人高兴的事/婚礼)

**话题核心**：事件类 - 婚礼 (Cousin's Wedding 表哥/表姐的婚礼)
**推荐适用话题**：
- A party you attended before
- A happy experience you had recently
- An important event that you celebrated
- A time that you saved money for something

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    I want to talk about my cousin's wedding for this topic. My cousin and I grew up together, so we are really close to each other. That's why I was quite excited about his wedding as well.

    The wedding was held in a fancy hotel which was located in the heart of the city, quite close to a park and CBD. Almost every one of our family and friends came to the wedding, people were dressed up and they couldn't wait to see the bride and groom.

    After a while, the wedding began, when I saw my cousin holding his wife's hand and walking down the aisle, I was truly happy for them and my tears almost came to my eyes. They stood on the stage and looked at each other with great affection. After giving their vows to each other, they exchanged the rings that symbolized their love.

    Later on, they walked down to the guests' seats and started toasting to their family and friends. All the guests stood up, welcomed them and gave them sincere blessings while tasting the quality wine.

    Seeing them standing so happily beside each other made me realize the meaning of marriage. Marriage is a turning point in one's life which brings many changes and responsibilities with it. From the bottom of my heart, I wish that they could have a loving and happy life forever.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    关于这个话题，我想谈谈我表哥的婚礼。我和我的表哥一起长大，所以我们彼此非常亲近。这也是为什么我对他的婚礼也感到非常兴奋。

    婚礼在一家豪华酒店举行，该酒店位于市中心，离一个公园和中央商务区（CBD）很近。几乎我们所有的家人和朋友都来参加了婚礼，人们盛装出席，迫不及待地想要见到新郎新娘。

    过了一会儿，婚礼开始了，当我看到我表哥牵着他妻子的手走下红毯时，我真心地为他们感到高兴，泪水几乎要流出来了。他们站在舞台上，深情地注视着彼此。在向对方宣誓后，他们交换了象征他们爱情的戒指。

    随后，他们走到宾客席前，开始向家人和朋友敬酒。所有的宾客都站起身来，欢迎他们，并在品尝美酒的同时送上真挚的祝福。

    看到他们如此幸福地并肩站在一起，让我意识到了婚姻的意义。婚姻是人生的一个转折点，它带来了许多变化和责任。我发自内心地祝愿他们能够永远拥有充满爱且幸福的生活。

## Topic 4: Describe a road trip / graduation trip (一次自驾/毕业旅行)

**话题核心**：事件类 - 毕业自驾游 (Kanas Lake Road Trip 喀纳斯湖自驾游)
**推荐适用话题**：
- A trip far from home
- An experience that you were scared
- A trip by public transport
- A perfect vacation idea
- A time when you changed your plan
- An occasion when you helped someone

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    I remember when I graduated from the university, some of my classmates came to a decision to visit the Kanas Lake on a road trip. Before the trip, everyone was assigned with certain tasks, mine was downloading some music for us to listen on the road.

    We started our trip on a fine Friday morning, some of the boys took turns to drive and others just chatted and sang along with the radio in the car. Every now and then, we stopped by and appreciated the views along the road. Of course, taking some pictures to capture that beautiful moment was absolutely a must do.

    After 9 hours' driving, we finally arrived at our destination. The sky was extremely blue and the lake was shining under the sun, we finally got ourselves away from the city crowd. We put up our tents first and prepared for lunch by ourselves. Even though the whole process was not as smooth as we expected, when we finally had a taste of the food we made, we were actually quite satisfied with it.

    Then it was time for climbing the mountain, at the middle of it, I felt really tired and didn't want to continue. But my friends encouraged me a lot and assisted me to climb onto the peak of the mountain. When I saw those beautiful views from the top, I felt that all the efforts were worth it. That also made me realize that most of the things we desire in life are hard to get but truly worth fighting for.

    I think the trip had given us a chance to strengthen our friendship before we left for different places after the graduation, it will definitely be a memory of life.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    我记得当我大学毕业时，我的一些同学决定去喀纳斯湖自驾游。旅行前，每个人都被分配了特定的任务，我的任务是下载一些在路上听的音乐。

    我们在一个晴朗的周五早上开始了旅程，男生们轮流开车，其他人就聊天，或者跟着车里的收音机唱歌。我们时不时地停下来欣赏沿途的风景。当然，拍些照片记录下那个美丽的瞬间绝对是必须要做的。

    开了9个小时的车后，我们终于到达了目的地。天空无比蔚蓝，湖面在阳光下熠熠生辉，我们终于远离了城市的喧嚣。我们先搭起帐篷，然后自己动手准备午餐。虽然整个过程并不像我们预期的那样顺利，但当我们终于尝到自己做的食物时，实际上我们都感到非常满足。

    接着就是爬山的时间了，爬到一半时，我感到非常累，不想继续了。但是我的朋友们一直在鼓励我，并帮助我爬到了山顶。当我从山顶俯瞰那些美景时，我觉得所有的付出都是值得的。这也让我意识到，我们在生活中渴望的大多数东西都是难以得到的，但确实值得为之奋斗。

    我认为这次旅行给我们提供了一个机会，在毕业后奔向不同地方之前巩固我们的友谊，这绝对会成为一生的记忆。

## Topic 5: Describe a gift you received / a useful object (一份有意义的礼物/手机)

**话题核心**：物品类 - 智能手机 (First Smartphone Nokia E63)
**推荐适用话题**：
- A gift you received
- A useful object you use daily
- A technology product that has made a difference in your life

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    Well, I still remember that I got my first smart phone when I turned 19 years old. My father gave it to me for celebrating my first part time job. It was not quite expensive but it still made a huge difference.

    It was a NOKIA E63 with a full keyboard, I could not get used to it when I first started using the phone, but it only took a few days for me to type as fast as before. Despite the use of keeping in touch with the people around me through calling and texting, the phone was equipped with many incredible functions as well.

    For instance, I am crazy about taking pictures, I love capturing the beautiful moments in my life. The phone had decent pixels so that I didn't have to borrow my father's heavy camera when it came to some special occasions like my birthday, campus Olympic and stuff.

    Another wonderful function is that I could start online payment with this phone. All I needed to do was to connect my bank account with my phone, and I didn't have to take cash and credit cards with me any more. No matter where I went, I could just pay the bills with my phone and it was absolutely the best thing for me since I hated putting all those changes in my pockets, it's really easy to lose them.

    I stopped using that phone a few years ago, but the first one is always the one that impressed me the most, and since it was the last phone that I needed my father to buy me, it was also kind of a connection between me and him I guess.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    好吧，我依然记得在我19岁时得到了我的第一部智能手机。我父亲送给我它是为了庆祝我的第一份兼职工作。它虽然不是很贵，但它仍然带来了巨大的改变。

    那是一部带有全键盘的诺基亚E63，我刚开始使用这部手机时还不太习惯，但我只花了几天的功夫，打字速度就和以前一样快了。除了通过打电话和发短信与周围的人保持联系这一用途之外，这部手机还配备了许多不可思议的功能。

    比如，我非常喜欢拍照，我喜欢记录我生活中的美好瞬间。这部手机的像素很不错，所以在遇到一些像我生日、校园运动会之类的特殊场合时，我就不用去借我父亲沉重的相机了。

    另一个极好的功能是，我可以用这部手机开始在线支付。我所需要做的就是将我的银行账户与我的手机绑定，这样我就不用再随身携带现金和信用卡了。无论我去哪里，我都可以直接用手机付账，这对我来说绝对是最好的一点，因为我讨厌把所有的零钱塞进兜里，它们真的很划不来还很容易弄丢。

    我几年前就停止使用那部手机了，但第一部手机总是给我留下最深印象的那一个，而且因为这是最后一部需要我父亲给我买的手机，我想它也算是我和他之间的一种纽带吧。

## Topic 6: Describe a second foreign language you want to learn (想学习的第二外语)

**话题核心**：学术/语言类 - 西班牙语 (Spanish)
**推荐适用话题**：
- A second foreign language you would like to learn in the future
- A course you want to study

### 📝 英文范文 (Model Essay)

    If I have to choose a language that I would like to learn in the future, Spanish is definitely the first thing that comes to my mind which I have been intending to learn for a long time.

    I still remember the first time I heard Spanish was from a song called "La isla bonita", which means the beautiful island, I was truly attracted by the melody and the lyrics of the song even though I couldn't even understand it at that time. Since then, I started to listen to a lot of Spanish songs and watch some Spanish movies, I was almost addicted to its amazing intonation and pronunciation, no wonder people regard Spanish as the language of God.

    Well, actually I have already started learning Spanish by myself. I bought some vocabulary books and downloaded a few apps on my phone to learn from the very basic things, but soon I realized that it was more than I could teach myself. Especially when it came to verbs, there were too many forms to master properly without any instructions, it gradually made me confused. Plus, there weren't any native speakers around me, I had nobody to practice with. Therefore, my plan of learning Spanish didn't go well like I expected.

    Another reason that I would love to learn Spanish is that it is also a worldwide language besides English, if I can ever have a chance to visit some of my favorite Latin American countries like Mexico and Colombia, Spanish will become extremely useful.

    Well, I really hope that someday I could have the opportunity to take a Spanish course and learn from a qualified teacher. I'm aware of the fact that there will be many difficulties during the process, but I believe where there is a will, there is a way.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译 (Chinese Translation)

    如果我必须选择一门我未来想学习的语言，西班牙语绝对是我的首选，我已经打算学习它很久了。

    我还记得第一次听到西班牙语是来自一首名叫《La Isla Bonita》（意为美丽岛）的歌曲，当时即使我连歌词都听不懂，也深深地被这首歌的旋律和歌词所吸引。从那以后，我开始听大量的西班牙语歌曲并看一些西班牙语电影，我几乎沉溺于它美妙的声调和发音中，难怪人们把西班牙语称为上帝的语言。

    好吧，其实我已经开始自己学习西班牙语了。我买了一些词汇书并在手机上下载了几个应用程序，从最基础的东西学起，但很快我就意识到这超出了我自学的范围。尤其是涉及到动词时，要在没有任何指导的情况下正确掌握太多的词形，这渐渐让我感到困惑。再加上我周围没有任何说母语的人，我没有人一起练习。因此，我的学习西班牙语计划并没有像我预期的那样顺利。

    我喜欢学习西班牙语的另一个原因在于，它是除了英语之外也是一种全球通用的语言。如果我能有机会去一些我最喜欢的拉丁美洲国家如墨西哥和哥伦比亚，西班牙语将变得极其有用。

    嗯，我真的希望有一天我能有机会参加西班牙语课程，并向合格的老师学习。我意识到在这个过程中会有很多困难，但我相信有志者，事竟成。
